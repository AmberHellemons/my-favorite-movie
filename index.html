The World of Magic

The Harry Potter universe is rich with magical creatures, spells, and locations that make it an unforgettable world. From Hogwarts to Diagon Alley, fans have grown attached to the many different settings where Harry's story unfolds.

In the films, these locations come to life through impressive special effects and art direction, bringing J.K. Rowling's imagination to the big screen.
